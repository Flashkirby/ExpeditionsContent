The main content addon for the Expeditions Base mod.

* 45 Main Quests provided by the Guide, up to Plantera.
* 46 Cycling daily quests.
* 43 Sidequests, up to Mechanical Bosses.
* Clerk NPC that sells expedition-unlockable items.
* Special expedition items that display extra Full Map information.
* Quest-unlocked early game weapon for Melee, Ranged, Magic and Summon.
